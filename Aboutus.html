BTS and ARMY: A Revolution in Fan Culture

Our Vision

  • Two-way Street, Global Community.
  • From Consumers to Co-creators.
  • Breaking the Mold, Redefining Fandom.

BTS and their fans, ARMY, have rewritten the fan culture rulebook. Forget one-way adoration! They're a global community, actively connected and co-creating. ARMY's passion fuels BTS's success, and together they champion self-love and social causes, making them a revolutionary force.

Powerhouse Septet

  1. RM (Kim Namjoon): The leader, known for his rapping skills and incredible intellect.
  2. Jin (Kim Seokjin): The eldest member, boasting impressive vocals and a hilarious personality.
  3. Suga (Min Yoongi): A rapper, songwriter, and producer with a sharp mind and smooth flow.
  4. J-Hope (Jung Hoseok): The dance machine with infectious positivity and sunshine-like energy.
  5. Jimin (Park Jimin): A captivating dancer and vocalist known for his dedication and powerful stage presence.
  6. V (Kim Taehyung): A soulful singer with a unique voice and a playful, artistic side.
  7. Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook): The youngest member, a multi-talented powerhouse with incredible vocals and dancing skills.
